Our Approach

Intention in
every step.

From the farm to your cup, each decision is deliberate. We never rush. The best things take time.

Step One

Source

Direct relationships with farms in Ethiopia, Colombia, and Guatemala. We visit origins yearly to taste, learn, and select.

Step Two

Roast

Small-batch roasting on a vintage Probat. Each profile is developed over weeks of tasting until we find the soul of the bean.

Step Three

Serve

Brewed fresh by baristas who care. We train for precision but serve with warmth. Every cup is an experience, not a transaction.

Signature Drinks

Made with intent.

Three drinks we keep coming back to. Simple ingredients, extraordinary care, honest flavor.

The Morning Ritual
Oat Latte · Ethiopia Yirgacheffe

Silky oat milk meets bright, floral Ethiopian beans. Notes of bergamot and honey with a velvet finish.

Double Ember
Espresso · Colombia Huila

Our house espresso, pulled long. Dark cherry, toasted walnut, and a clean caramel sweetness that lingers.

Oak Cold Brew
Cold Brew · Guatemala Antigua

Steeped 18 hours in oak barrels. Smooth, chocolatey, with a whisper of vanilla and stone fruit.
