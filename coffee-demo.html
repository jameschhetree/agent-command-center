The Philosophy
We don't make coffee
for people in a rush.
We make it for those
who linger.
There's a certain slowness that coffee demands. A ritual of heat, of patience, of paying attention to the moment the bloom releases. We built Ember & Oak around that cadence.

The Process
Three Stages
01
Source

Direct relationships with farms in Ethiopia, Colombia, and Guatemala. We visit every harvest. We know the altitude, the soil, the family.

Farm to Roaster
02
Roast

Small-batch roasting in a 1962 Probat. We listen to the crack, watch the color shift, pull at precisely the right second.

12kg Batches
03
Serve

Brewed within days of roasting. Every method calibrated to the bean. Pour-over, espresso, cold drip -- each one deliberate.
