Submission Strategy

7-Step Playlist Placement Playbook

  1. 1 Start with free platforms -- Soundplate, Indiemono, PlaylistPartner, and DailyPlaylists cost nothing and build initial traction.
  2. 2 Spend $20-50 on SubmitHub targeting alt R&B, neo-soul, and chill R&B curators for guaranteed listens and quality feedback.
  3. 3 DM curators on Instagram with a personal message -- mention a specific playlist you love and why Noah fits. No copy-paste.
  4. 4 Submit 4-6 weeks before release date to give curators time to review, schedule, and add the track before launch day.
  5. 5 Target curators who already feature Daniel Caesar, Brent Faiyaz, SZA, Steve Lacy, and Frank Ocean -- genre alignment is everything.
  6. 6 Build from small curators up to large -- small placements create streaming momentum that makes large curators more likely to accept.
  7. 7 Track every placement in a spreadsheet and re-submit to curators who said yes on previous releases -- relationships compound.
